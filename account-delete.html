Delete Your Account

How to delete your account

If you want to delete your NTA SkyNav account and all associated data, please send an email to:

info@ntaskynav.app

Click the button above to open your email app with the request pre-filled.

What happens next?

  • We will process your request within 48 hours.
  • All your personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  • This includes your email address, profile data, flight logs, and any other stored information.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion or your privacy, contact us at info@ntaskynav.app.

See our Privacy Policy for more information about how we handle your data.
