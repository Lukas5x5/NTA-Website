⚠ iOS Login Issue

Login problems? Here's a quick fix.

Some of you may experience login issues on iOS. This happens when you selected “Hide My Email” during the Apple Sign-In process. Apple then creates a random, encrypted email address — which prevents the app from logging you in correctly.

 

How to fix it

Follow these steps to reset and fix your login:

1

Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad

2

Go to Passwords

3

Search for NTA SkyNav and tap on it

4

Tap Edit, then Delete Password

5

Open NTA SkyNav and sign in again with Apple

6

Important: This time, do NOT select “Hide My Email” — use your real email address instead

 

Why does this happen?

When you choose “Hide My Email”, Apple generates a random relay address (e.g. abc123@privaterelay.appleid.com). This encrypted email doesn't match your actual account, which causes the login to fail. Using your real email solves this.
